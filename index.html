Samir Karam

Github: SamirVK

I am a PhD student in the department of Mathematics and Statistics at Concordia University under the supervision of Simone Brugiapaglia and Galia Dafni. I have worked extensively in the field of numerical solvers for partial differential equations and scientific machine learning, which involved using neural networks and “Spectral Collocation” methods to solve a class of reaction-diffusion equations in high-dimensions.

Research Interests

  • Numerical solvers for PDEs
  • High-dimensional function approximation
  • Scientific ML
